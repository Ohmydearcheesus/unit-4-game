Crystal Collector!

Each of the four gems hide a number. Click one of the gems to find out how what number to add to your current number count!

Your goal is to achieve the same number as the target count. But be careful! If you end up with too many points, you'll lose the round!

If you should wish to throw away all of your current records of wins and losses, just press 'Start Over' to begin anew!
